I Saw A Child

I saw a child who couldn't walk,
sit on a horse, laugh and talk.
Then ride it through a field of daisies
and yet he couldn't walk unaided.

I saw a child, no legs below,
sit on a horse, and make it go,
through the woods of green
and places he had never been
to sit and stare, except from a chair.

I saw a child who could only crawl,
mount a horse and sit up tall.
Put it through degrees of paces
and laugh at the wonder in our faces.

take up and hold the reins of life
and that same child was heard to say

Annual General Meeting 2025

Oct 4, 2025 | time 2 pm

The Annual General Meeting (AGM) for Thornton Rose RDA will take place on: Saturday, the 4th of October 2025 at 2 pm Thornton Rose RDA, Thornton Farm, Rosewell, EH24 9E All members and their families are welcome to attend, or members can send in their proxy votes via email

The following links can be used to view AGM 2025 Agenda and previous minutes for the 2024 AGM. As per notification by email, the Agenda contains voting information that requires attention whether you are attending the AGM or not. Also, please let Charlotte Smyth (Secretary) know if you would like other matters discussed at the AGM or Trustees Meetings.
